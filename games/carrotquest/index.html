One of my first games.

Carrot Quest is a very short platformer game created by yours truly, and some other friends.
I had high expectations and it eventually became too big, and too much for me to handle.

I refuse to finish or work on this game any longer, as It was merely a project for friends to enjoy. Never truly meant for the general public.

I released it to the public anyways, in it's full unfinished state. I never saw any success from it, nor saw success ever coming from it.

The version that exists for download here, is purely for archive purposes.
